## Project Summary

Enhancement to Techcombank's digital account opening process to include integrated eKYC (electronic Know Your Customer) verification. This will enable customers to open accounts entirely through mobile app without visiting a branch.

### Goals

1. Enable 100% digital account opening without branch visit
2. Reduce account opening time from 30 minutes to < 5 minutes
3. Improve customer satisfaction scores (NPS + 20 points)
4. Reduce fraud rate to < 0.1% through enhanced eKYC

### Key Features

- **Biometric Verification** — Face matching with ID photo
- **Document Scanning** — Automated document extraction and validation
- **Real-time KYC** — Instant verification against government databases
- **Risk Scoring** — AI-based customer risk assessment
- **Automated Approval** — < 5% of accounts require manual review
