## Project Summary

Development of an AI-powered conversational chatbot to provide 24/7 customer support and handle routine banking inquiries. The chatbot will handle account inquiries, payment assistance, product information, and escalate complex issues to human agents.

### Goals

1. Reduce support ticket volume by 40%
2. Provide 24/7 customer support availability
3. Achieve 85%+ first-contact resolution rate
4. Improve customer satisfaction scores
5. Reduce support cost per interaction

### Key Capabilities

- **Account Inquiries** — Balance, transaction history, account details
- **Payment Assistance** — Payment initiation, troubleshooting failed payments
- **Product Information** — Product features, rates, eligibility criteria
- **Complaint Handling** — Complaint registration and tracking
- **Human Escalation** — Seamless handoff to support agents
- **Multi-language** — Vietnamese and English support
