## Overview

The Wealth Management domain provides investment and portfolio management services for Techcombank's high-net-worth and mass-affluent customers. This includes investment product distribution, portfolio management, wealth advisory services, and comprehensive wealth reporting.

### Key Responsibilities

- **Portfolio Management** — Management of customer investment portfolios
- **Fund Distribution** — Distribution of mutual funds, stocks, bonds, and structured products
- **Advisory Platform** — Wealth advisory and financial planning tools
- **Wealth Reporting** — Comprehensive portfolio and performance reporting
- **Product Management** — Investment product selection and curation

## Architecture

This domain integrates with:

- **Core Banking Domain** — Account master, payment services, GL posting
- **Payments Domain** — Fund transfers for investments
- **Digital Channels Domain** — Investment application interface
- **Data Platform Domain** — Portfolio analytics and performance reporting
- **Risk Management Domain** — Investment risk assessment

## Technology Stack

**Current Technologies:**
- **Portfolio System**: Temenos Multifonds (investment management)
- **Market Data**: Bloomberg, Reuters feeds
- **Analytics**: Python (performance analytics), R (quantitative models)
- **Reporting**: Tableau, custom dashboards
- **APIs**: REST services for third-party integrations
